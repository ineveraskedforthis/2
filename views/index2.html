Welcome to my island, child of SIR.
I will help you to get used to local customs.
Let's visit local market first
(Press Market button on top to continue)
Here you can see local "Sell orders".
When local people want to sell something, they go to the local market and open their shop
You don't have any money to buy anything right now, so let's go to the requests board.
(Press "Sell orders" tab to continue)
Here you can see local "Buy orders".
When local people people need something, they go to the market and put their requests on this board.
Main way to earn money on this island is to gather materials and sell them to these people
Make sure to read these orders carefully: some of them could be quite easy to fulfill.
Now, go to local craftsmen
(Press "Equipment" tab to continue)
Here you can buy equipment created by local masters.
To create armour and weapons they need raw materials.
If you want them to provide better items, make sure to complete some of the raw goods requests.
Let's review your skills.
(Press "Skills" button to continue)
Not impressive right now.
You can improve your skills in two ways: Learn by doing things which use the skill. Or pay for private tutoring.
Now, let's take a quick look at things you can craft
(Press "Craft" button to continue)
In this list you can see items you can craft.
Your skills are fairly low, so you can't craft high quality items.
But as you are really poor and can't afford buying good weapon, consider gathering some wood to make yourself a spear or a mace.
(You can gather materials via buttons in the lower left corner)
Now you are ready for the adventure. Go.
(Press Map button to complete the tutorial)
